Formula,

C=2 πε0 (ε1h1 + ε2h2) / ln (r2/r1)

Where,

C = Capacitance in µF
r1 = radius of inner cylinder/pipe for ‘pipe in pipe’ type probe
r2 = radius of outer cylinder/pipe for ‘pipe in pipe’ type probe
ε0 = permittivity of the space = 8.85 x 10-12
ε1 = permittivity of air =1
ε2= Dielectric constant of the process fluid/selected service
h1 =Height (Level) of air column = span - liqiud column height
h2 =Height (Level) of liquid column
span = (Tank height * 0.9) - 5
